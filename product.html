Blister Foil

Blister Foil

Width ≤ 980 mm
Core ID 76 mm/152 mm
Slit rolls OD Customisable as per customer’s requirement
Download Data Sheet

Trefoil’s blister foil is high-quality, hygroscopic aluminum packaging material suitable for pills, capsules, and tablets. It comes with 25-28 micron thickness and various anti-counterfeit features like UV inks, thermochromic inks, and color-shifting inks. This foil is coated with FDA approved materials making them food safe and eco-friendly.

Get a quote

Top Features

  • Superior barrier protection against water vapor, gasses, and light for product freshness and longevity.
  • Exceptional formability and high malleability for deep drawing blister cavities and unique designs.
  • High thermal stability and resistance to delamination for reliable performance in various temperatures.
  • Maximum fracture-free forming capabilities for smooth and seamless packaging appearance.
  • Extended shelf life for products, reducing waste and keeping them fresher for longer.
  • Ideal for cold-forming applications, making it a versatile choice for packaging needs.

Application

CFB is ideal for packaging different types of drugs/medicine with high-sensitive formulations.

CFB Variants

  • 25 µ OPA - 45 µ soft aluminum foil - 60 µ PVC with primer
  • 25 µ OPA - 50 µ soft aluminum foil - 60 µ PVC
  • 25 µ OPA - 50 µ soft aluminum foil - 60 µ PVC with primer
  • 25 µ OPA - 60 µ soft aluminum foil - 60 µ PVC
  • 25 µ OPA - 50 µ soft aluminum foil - 100 µ PVC
